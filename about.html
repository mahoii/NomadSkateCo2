Our Story

Nomad Skate Co. was founded in October 1999, selling authentically creative decks to people of all ages from the humble town of Urban Park. Skateboarding is more than a sport to us, it’s a canvas waiting to be painted by your individuality and a vibrant community welcoming everyone.

Our Mission

Our main goal is to make high quality skateboards that represent different identities and grow the skating community. We hope you chose us to start your skateboarding journey!

Meet the Team
